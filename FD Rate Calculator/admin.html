Rate Calculation Preview

This shows how rates are calculated using the current business parameters:

1. Calculate Oncost Amount:
   Oncost Amount = (Gross Salary × Oncost Percentage) ÷ 100

2. Calculate Salary + Oncost:
   Salary + Oncost = Gross Salary + Oncost Amount

3. Calculate Individual Overhead Recovery:
   Individual Overhead = (Salary + Oncost) × Overhead Recovery Factor

4. Calculate Total (Salary + Oncost + Overhead):
   Total = Salary + Oncost + Individual Overhead

5. Calculate Markup:
   Markup = Total × (Markup Percentage ÷ 100)

6. Calculate Total Amount to be Recovered:
   Amount to Recover = Total + Markup

7. Calculate Hourly Rate:
   Hourly Rate = Amount to Recover ÷ Annual Billable Hours
